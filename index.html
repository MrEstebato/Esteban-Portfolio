👋 About Me

Creative Coder with a passion for learning

💻 Passionate Developer 📚 Engineering Student 📘 Lifelong Learner

I am Esteban Leal Menéndez, current student at Tecnológico de Monterrey, studying Computer Technology Engineering. I like programming and anything to do with computers. I was on the Tecbot 3158 robotics team where I learned to program in Java and various mechanical things and due to my fast learning capabilities and dexterity I got to be part of the drive team. Later I was on the High Altitude 9280 robotics team as a programming mentor. Both participating in the FRC competition. I have participated in a Hack'n'jam where my team and I came in 4th place; and a Hackathon where my team and I won first place with an inclusive project based on sign language.

Talking more about me, I really like learning and meeting people, that's why I'm in the representative band of the campus playing electric guitar. I like to play sports, specifically team sports like soccer and volleyball, although I also like to play tennis. I love playing video games and watching movies.
